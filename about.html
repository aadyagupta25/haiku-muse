About Haiku Muse

Welcome to the story behind Haiku Muse, a labor of love from dedicated haiku enthusiasts committed to creating a global community for this ancient poetic form. Our journey began with a simple idea: to create a comprehensive resource that bridges traditional haiku scholarship with modern digital accessibility.

The Story of Haiku Muse

Haiku Muse was born from the shared passion of two literature students, Aadya Gupta and Jayeeta Sharma, who discovered the transformative power of haiku during their academic studies. What began as a mutual appreciation for this poetic form evolved into a vision for creating an inclusive space where both scholarly analysis and creative expression could flourish.

In 2025, what started as a small digital repository of haiku resources for fellow students has grown into a comprehensive platform that serves haiku enthusiasts worldwide. We believe that the beauty of haiku lies in its accessibility—how it distills complex human experiences into just a few powerful lines. Similarly, we've designed Haiku Muse to distill extensive scholarship into accessible, engaging content for everyone from casual readers to dedicated poets and academics.

Today, Haiku Muse stands as a testament to our belief that literary traditions thrive when communities form around them. We've created this space not just as a repository of knowledge, but as a living, breathing community where the ancient art of haiku continues to evolve in the digital age.
